Our Story

1.1

STEM Tutoring was founded with one main goal, to ensure every child, regardless of their background, achieve excellent results in STEM related subjects, at an unbelievably, affordable cost. Having worked at two leading independent schools, our founder, Jaspal Singh, was keen to share his expertise with schools across the UK in his role as a Subject Matter Expert for the National Centre for Computing Education and worked with over 400 schools in the UK supporting the development of the KS3 – KS5 computing curriculum. Having seen first hand, the disparity, in the quality and delivery of the computing curriculum across the country, he started a series of one week free summer bootcamps covering the GCSE Computer Science syllabus from 2019 to 2022. This led to the start of STEM Tutoring. An online tutoring company, unlike any other tutoring company in the world. For the cost of one session with a traditional private tutor, we provide students with world class teachers from leading schools in the UK, access to a 1 year programme of study with 2 hours of weekly live lessons.

Many families think that they are limited to the schools around them until they discover our range of courses from GCSEs to Super Curricular Activities with accreditation from the British Science Association with the Crest Awards

Why STEM ?

1.2

As we move into the future, the demand for skilled workers in science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) fields is only increasing. This trend is expected to continue, with many industries relying on a well-trained STEM workforce to drive innovation and progress.

For students, this means that pursuing a strong foundation in STEM subjects during their school years can pay off significantly in the long run. At the GCSE level, building a strong foundation in STEM subjects can not only open up a wide range of career opportunities, but it can also give students the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in higher education and beyond.

For students, this means that pursuing a strong foundation in STEM subjects during One way to help GCSE students build this foundation is through STEM tuition. By providing targeted, personalized instruction in STEM subjects, tuition can help students develop a deeper understanding of these important topics and gain the confidence they need to succeed in their studies..

But the benefits of STEM tuition go beyond just improving grades and test scores. By working with a tutor, students can also develop valuable skills such as problem-solving, critical thinking, and communication, which are all essential for success in any field.

In addition to helping students succeed academically, STEM tuition can also provide a valuable opportunity for students to explore their interests and discover their passion for these subjects. With the guidance of an experienced tutor, students can learn about the exciting career opportunities available in STEM fields and start planning for their future. Overall, STEM tuition is an essential investment for GCSE students in 2023 and beyond. By providing personalized instruction and helping students develop a strong foundation in these subjects, tuition can help students succeed in their studies and pave the way for a rewarding career in a STEM field.

Computer science Doesn't Have To Be Difficult

1.3

Computer science is known for being one of the most challenging subjects with poorer results than other subjects since 2016 with a declining uptake and a gender imbalance in the classroom. However, we believe computer science is a fundamental subject in teaching problem solving and critical thinking skills, which are essential for employability. Most students struggle with understanding the programming constructs within Computer Science and find themselves lost in the Pseudocode Language or Exam Reference Language. That's why we're here to help you transform your approach to computer science from anxiety to achievement!

Throughout our tutoring sessions we focus on understanding each topic to the fullest extent, as we understand the connectivity of topics in Computer Science. The main focus of the program is to prepare students for their final exams in May/ June. This is achieved by extensively practicing an exhaustive list of programming exam questions followed by weekly theory based live sessions.

How Master Classes Work

  • Each lesson is dedicated to covering both theoretical notes and exam practice questions. As part of the lesson, we go through a number of exam questions, exploring what the exam board requires of students and how to score the highest marks.
  • Learners are supported beyond the course through the provision of high-quality, comprehensive and original materials, including notes and question booklets. These resources have been specially created to supplement the learning experience and support students beyond the online course.
  • The live lessons are held over Microsoft Teams and Zoom with plenty of opportunity for student engagement, including live quizzes and Q&A’s.
  • Students that are not able to join a full session are able to access the recording and work at their own pace using the class materials which are accessible via Google Drive and MS Teams.
